About Me

My name is Dominique and I am a Web Development Student based out of the US. I enjoy the creativity and logic that goes into making presentable, user-friendly websites and applications.

I started teaching myself how to code in 2020. As of 2023, I am a current student at Fullstack Academy's Web Development Program. I am excited about increasing my knowledge and skills in order to become a successful web developer.
